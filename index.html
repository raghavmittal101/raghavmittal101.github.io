RM

I am a researcher at TCS Research with a strong focus on Virtual Reality, Mixed Reality, and Generative AI applications for immersive environments. My work bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI technologies and intuitive 3D content creation in XR.

I completed my MS (by Research) in Computer Science Engineering from IIIT Hyderabad, where I developed novel locomotion techniques for VR environments. My research interests include 3D scene composition, sketch-based shape retrieval, and multi-modal interaction in extended reality.

Currently seeking PhD positions to advance research in immersive technologies and human-computer interaction. I have published multiple papers at premier venues including WACV, ISMAR, and VRST, and hold patents in VR locomotion and 3D shape retrieval.
